Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a bespoke piece take?

Our bespoke process typically takes 6-12 weeks from initial consultation to final fitting. This timeline allows us to handcraft every detail with precision and perfection.

What is included in the initial consultation?

Your consultation includes a detailed discussion of your vision, body measurements, fabric selection, design customization, and timeline. We take time to understand your style, occasion, and expectations.

Can I request custom modifications?

Absolutely. Every piece at Ayshamay Atelier is bespoke. We work with you to create designs that reflect your personality, heritage, and vision. Modifications are part of our creative process.

What fabrics do you use?

We use premium fabrics including luxury aso-oke, fine lace, silk blends, and carefully selected textiles that honor Nigerian textile traditions while meeting world-class quality standards.

How do I care for my Ayshamay piece?

Each piece comes with detailed care instructions. We recommend gentle hand washing, professional dry cleaning, and proper storage. Your garment is an investmentâ€”we'll help you maintain it beautifully.

Do you offer rush orders?

Rush orders can be accommodated on a case-by-case basis. Contact us directly to discuss your timeline, and we'll provide options. Premium rush fees may apply.
